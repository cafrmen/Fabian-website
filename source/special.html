Patch XR

Patch XR is a native VR environment to build and play electronic instruments in a 3D space. This duet was recorded in October 2020, one part in Mexico City by Ensemble Liminar, and the other in Geneva by Ensemble Contrechamps. Recordings were made separately, and brought together in the same virtual space. This is part of a research in the use of VR in new and experimental music.

Ghost Variations

In the Museo Nacional de la Estampa (Mexico) the artist Omar Barquet made a piece of engraving which represents his graphic interpretation of Schumann's work titled Geistervariationen. As part of the presentation of the piece at the Museum, he asked two musicians to carry out, as a random game, a sound re-interpretation of his work in a performance, allowing a space where the performer tries to transmute the abstracted graphics to music with a certain freedom of interpretation.
