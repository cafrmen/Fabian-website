Fabian Campuzano

Recordings Collaborations

Tuba Player, Performer, Sound Designer and Creator.

Focused on exploration and experimentation with sound, he has three main projects.

Tuba + Live Electronics, Sound Design and React! His most ambitious project, is currently in the research stage and tries to make the music react to what the performer is doing, regardless of whether the performer is a musician, an artist from another discipline or public.

He was a professor in three professional institutions and Scenic Creator of FONCA in his country, Mexico. He has performed in Mexico, Chile, Canada, South Korea, Croatia, Germany and the Netherlands.
